Data Science Portfolio

The below highlights represent key projects in my data science portfolio, cultivated through personal and academic endeavors. The link to the entire GitHub repos is as follows: https://github.com/harrygarwood/Data-Science-Portfolio.git

Machine Learning Analysis of Student Achievements in England

I utilised data from the GOV.UK Explore Education Statistics site to perform a machine learning analysis, exploring factors affecting student achievements in England.

Machine Learning Exploration of Factors Affecting Student Retention

In this project, I performed a machine learning analysis to explore factors influencing student retention, using data from a UK education provider.

Strategic Dashboard - Student Achievement and Retention Rates

I created a Power BI dashboard designed to provide a strategic overview of student achievement and retention rates for an educational institution.

Analysing the Success of the UK Covid-19 Vaccination Strategy

I compared the effectiveness of the UK's Covid-19 vaccination strategy to Denmark and Italy, focusing on vaccination rates, deaths, and hospitalisations. Additionally, I analysed the performance by the regions of the UK. The study provides valuable insights into the diverse impacts of vaccination strategies, contributing to the broader conversation on global pandemic response.

Analysis of 16-18 Local Authority Level Destinations

I conducted an in-depth analysis of the '16-18 Local Authority Level Destinations' dataset from the UK Government, focusing on the post-education destinations of 16-18-year-old students in England. The study provides valuable insights into various destination types, showcasing trends and patterns within the education landscape.

Designing a Relational Database for Classroom Observations

I designed a relational database dedicated to recording and reporting school classroom observations for a specific education provider. Additionally, a comprehensive report detailing the benefits derived from the implementation of the proposed relational database has been created.
