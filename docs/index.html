Analysing the Success of the UK Covid-19 Vaccination Strategy

I compared the effectiveness of the UK's Covid-19 vaccination strategy to Denmark and Italy, focusing on vaccination rates, deaths, and hospitalisations. Additionally, I analysed the peformance by the regions of the UK. The study provides valuable insights into the diverse impacts of vaccination strategies, contributing to the broader conversation on global pandemic response.
